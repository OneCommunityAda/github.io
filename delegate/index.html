How to delegate

Let's grow together! :)

Yoroi Desktop


1. Access the Yoroi Wallet official website: yoroi-wallet.com.
2. Click the download button to open the combo list and select your preferred browser.
3. Run the setup to install Yoroi Wallet. It will be installed as a plugin to your chosen browser.
4. Launch the Yoroi wallet plugin within your browser.
5. Follow the instructions to connect your hardware wallet, create a new wallet, or restore an existing one.
6. Go to the “Delegation list” tab, and search for “1COMM”.
7. Select 1COMM stake pool and click the “Delegate” button.
8. Finish the delegation process by confirming the delegation registration transaction.

Notes:
- It will take at least 15-20 days to receive your first staking reward.
- You will be able to track your rewards in the "Dashboard" tab.

Yoroi Mobile


1. Go to Google Play (for Android) or Apple Store (for iOS) and install the Yoroi mobile app for your device.
2. Follow the instructions to connect your hardware wallet, create a new wallet, or restore an existing one.
3. Go to the “Dashboard” tab and click “Go to Staking Center”.
4. Search for “1COMM” and select 1COMM stake pool.
5. Click the “Delegate” button.
6. Finish the delegation process by confirming the delegation registration transaction.

Notes:
- It will take at least 15-20 days to receive your first staking reward.
- You will be able to track your rewards in the "Dashboard" tab.

Daedalus



1. Access the Daedalus Wallet official website: daedaluswallet.io.
2. Daedalus is a full node wallet. As such you will have to wait for Daedalus to sync it's data with the blockchain.
3. After Daedalus finished syncing, follow the instructions to connect your hardware wallet, create a new wallet, or restore an existing one.
4. Click the second button in the left panel and select the “Stake Pools” tab.
5. Search for “1COMM” and select 1COMM stake pool.
6. Click the “Delegate to this pool” button.
7. Finish the delegation process by confirming the delegation registration transaction.

Notes:
- It will take at least 15-20 days to receive your first staking reward.
- You will be able to track your rewards in the "Rewards" section.

ADA Lite


WARNING: As ADA Lite is an online wallet, the only recommended access method for ADA Lite is using hardware wallet. If you use mnemonic or key file on ADA Lite, your key information will have to be sent over Internet to reach the server.

1. Access the ADA Lite website: adalite.io.
2. Choose your preferred access method to your Cardano wallet. (Highly recommend using ADA Lite only if you use a hardware wallet.)
3. Click the “Staking” tab.
4. Paste the pool ID 75f7b7dec6e4c973407a6a64132961635f405f2c3a9ff31598db01da in the "Stake Pool ID" field and click the “Delegate” button.
5. Finish the delegation process by confirming the delegation registration transaction.

Note:
- It will take at least 15-20 days to receive your first staking reward.

Compare Wallets

General notes
Not your keys, not your coins: The person owning private keys is the one deciding how the crypto assets associated are spent – if you don’t own this, you’re entrusting your crypto to a third party (ex: an exchange).
Secure your mnemonic phrase: Do not lose your mnemnonic phrase. It is a sequence of 12, 15, 24 or 27 words that is used to generate the private key. Store it safely, and always bear in mind that with this phrase you gain full control over your funds.
Hardware Wallet: All listed options (Yoroi, Daedalus and ADA Lite) support hardware wallet (Nano Ledger S/X or Trezor). Using a hardware wallet is the most secure way of storing your crypto as the private key is never exposed online.
Comparison
Yoroi Desktop or Mobile: Light weight wallet, for normal users.
Yoroi is EMURGO's one-click-install, light wallet for Cardano. With Yoroi, it is not necessary to download a copy of the blockchain’s history. This makes it a simple, fast way to store and transact your ada (₳), and secure on the same time as your private keys are encrypted and never shared with Yoroi servers or third party providers. Yoroi available as either a web-based extension or a downloadable app.
Daedalus: Full node wallet, recommended for advanced users.
Daedalus is a full-node desktop wallet developed by IOG (Input Output Global). Daedalus is unique in that it downloads a full copy of the Cardano blockchain and independently validates every transaction in its history. This provides operations without a centrally hosted third-party server.
ADA Lite: Light weight wallet.
ADA Lite is a open-source client-side interface for direct interaction with the Cardano blockchain developed by Vaccuum Labs. In this case, the only recommended access method is with hardware wallet. This is not a browser plugin as in the case of Yoroi, but a website. In this case, if you access with mnemonic or key file, bear in mind that your private key will be transmitted over the Internet, even though encrypted.
